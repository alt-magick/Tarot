Major Arcana

1. The Fool – You may feel pressure to decide, but don’t rush. Trust your judgment as you step into something new.


2. The Magician – As you move forward, your dedication and willingness to help others will bring pride and steady progress.


3. The High Priestess – Patience is especially important in love; allow feelings to develop naturally.


4. The Empress – Make the most of what you have—your financial efforts are beginning to show results.


5. The Emperor – If tension appears in a close relationship, calm communication will restore balance.


6. The Hierophant – Your practical wisdom with money attracts opportunity, but saving what you have keeps you secure.


7. The Lovers – Avoid jumping to conclusions; change can happen peacefully without conflict.


8. The Chariot – A calmer phase is approaching, giving you space to regain clarity and direction.


9. Strength – In this steadiness, your generosity shines—just avoid expecting too much in return.


10. The Hermit – Reflect carefully on how you spend your energy and focus on what supports your long-term goals.


11. Wheel of Fortune – A new relationship or meaningful restart is possible; be ready to commit sincerely.


12. Justice – You can reason through challenges—slow, steady progress brings success.


13. The Hanged Man – Financial security strengthens as you take initiative and ask for help when needed.


14. Death – Trust your abilities more deeply; transformation is guiding you forward.


15. Temperance – Sudden changes may arise, but flexibility allows you to adapt wisely.


16. The Devil – Your independence is strong—hold your principles while remaining open to connection.


17. The Tower – Others may look to you for leadership; set a steady example.


18. The Star – Hard work continues, yet completion brings satisfaction and renewed hope.


19. The Moon – Even during uncertainty, love for family provides grounding and joy.


20. The Sun – A new opportunity appears—stay observant and ready to act.


21. Judgement – You are ready for meaningful change; explore new goals with confidence.


22. The World – Financial discipline and steady effort now build lasting security and completion.
